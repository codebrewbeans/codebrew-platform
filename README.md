# codebrew-platform
🚀 Level up your grind. A gamified coffee experience for developers and gamers. From Codebrew roasts to Logic Quests.

CodeBrew Platform – Fuel for Devs
The first coffee shop where your logic pays the bills. > Stop debugging on empty—solve challenges, earn XP, and unlock real-world coffee rewards.

I’m a 19-year-old dev from Germany, and I spent the last few months building CodeBrew solo. Why? Because I was tired of generic coffee brands. I wanted a brand that speaks our language—not just with cool stickers, but through action.

At CodeBrew, coffee isn't just a commodity; it's the reward for the work.

⚔️ The Concept: No Gimmicks, Just Logic
Forget boring loyalty points. Here’s how you level up your setup:

Solve Coding Challenges: Algorithms, Bit Manipulation, and Logic puzzles.

Earn XP: Gain experience with every solved challenge.

Unlock Rewards: Leveling up grants you permanent discounts (up to 20%) on our roasts like "BUGFIX".

🗺️ Roadmap & Community Rewards (v1.0 -> v2.0)
The grind doesn't stop at discounts. As the community grows, I’m expanding the loot table:

XP-Based Gear: Exclusive "Level 50" mugs and apparel you can't buy—only earn.

Coffee Drops: Community-wide events where top-tier players get their fuel on the house.

Global Quests: Collaborative challenges to unlock new roasts for the entire shop.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Curious how a one-man startup runs?

Frontend: Next.js & Tailwind CSS

Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL & Auth)

Deployment: Vercel

Logic: Custom challenge-evaluation engine.
